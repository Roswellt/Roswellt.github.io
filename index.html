Bus Texter

A python tool that texts which bus number and route to take to get from location to destination to your mobile number.

Built using the GMail and Google maps API. The Google maps API is used to get a bus route from location to destination, which is then formatted into a readable output

The GMail API receives incoming mobile texts with location and destination specified, and then sends the Google maps results through an email which is sent as a mobile text

Automify

Developed with a group of friends during the future of work hackathon. A hackathon that focuses on the future automation of work by artificial intelligence and automation.

Our project was designed to give prospective job seekers a way to easily identify the likelihood of a job being automated during their job search.

A chrome extension that displays the likelihood of automation of a job posting on the Indeed search engine. The data was taken from a dataset that was made available by the hackathon. Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
